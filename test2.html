Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract or 1 vanilla bean
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla extract and stir it.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually stir into the wet mixture.
  5. Finally, add in the chocolate chips and fold the mixture.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto baking paper and add few chocolate chips on top of each ball.
  7. Bake for 9 to 12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  8. Cool the cookies for 5 minutes.

Enjoy your cookies!!!!!
